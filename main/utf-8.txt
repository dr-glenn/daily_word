              <h2>What It Means</h2>
              <p>A pseudonym is a name that someone (such as a writer) uses instead of their real name.</p>

<p>// <a href="https://www.britannica.com/biography/bell-hooks">bell hooks</a> is the <em>pseudonym</em> of the American writer Gloria Jean Watkins.</p>
